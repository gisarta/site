GIS "ARTA"

automated command and control system

It has been used by the Armed Forces of Ukraine since 2014 and has shown high efficiency compared to traditional approaches to management and control.

Most widely itâ€™s used in artillery units, due to the specifics of planning and conducting hostilities, the requirements for factual data and the urgency of obtaining information about the results of hostilities.

At the same time, GIS Arta has proven itself as a good instrument for situational and control centers, and exceptional tool for planning, monitoring, processing, and disseminating the results of intelligence operations.

The system is being developed and modernized based on close work and constant consultations with the users at the forefront of combat operations.

Purpose of the system

  • Rapid deployment of the communication network between intelligence facilities, decision-making centers (headquarters) and means of destruction
  • Ensuring the situational awareness
  • Maintaining a catalog of spatial objects and thematic maps with limited access
  • Ensuring processes of working with targets at different levels from receiving from different sources, to planning the defeat and distribution between the appropriate means
  • Coordination of target detection, tracking and defeat systems
  • Artillery systems and means of reconnaissance placement planning;
  • Calculation of firing parameters for all types of defeat means;
  • Minimization response time in counter-battery mode;
  • Integration of various technical systems and complexes of intelligence and targeting of both domestic and foreign production;
  • Minimization of radio usage and increasing the level of communications secrecy;
  • Sophisticated geospatial tools for various logistics and engineering tasks, problems of orientation, positioning and localization in the field, planning the placement of observation points or building a communication system based on line of sight, etc .

Cartography

  • Ready-to-use basemap with the ability to download new maps and layers
  • Support for various coordinate systems and conversion between them
  • Standard map tools, including various geospatial measurements, working with additional layers and scenes, objects on the map, etc.
  • Search and navigation to administrative-territorial objects, objects of situation, by coordinates, etc.
  • Support for altitude information
  • Elevation profiles and visibility zones (panorama) support
  • Unified system of objects designation and support of universal classification standards, including NATO standards

Communication Network

  • Building a decentralized mash-network, including algorithms for automatic search and traversal of failed network nodes, creating new routes for organizing data exchange, etc.
  • Reliable protection of the data transmitting channel
  • Automatic data synchronization and messaging between network nodes
  • Flexible configuration of data access permissions
  • Encryption of all data transmitted over the network, including objects, text messages and voice communication, etc.
  • Low network bandwidth requirements

Cybersecurity

  • Double encrypted data transmission channel
  • Encryption of each individual message by recipient' certificate
  • Encryption of all information transmitted between levels of subordination.
  • Mutual consent is required prior to any data transfer
  • Flexible configuration of access permissions and visibility zones for all objects and subjects of the system
  • Ability to block access for any individual device within 10 minutes of receiving a request
